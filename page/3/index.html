what is linked data part 1

Last weekend, I got to attend the NYC Archives Unconference (held by the wonderful archivists at Barnard College). It was a great day, although I admit that I skipped out at points to work on Columbia’s test BIBFRAME Editor (more on that later). In a minute of cheek, I proposed to lead a session on Linked Data - and I hoped it would be the kind of Linked Data session that I’m always looking for, by which I mean one based on real world examples. Since these examples-based and libraries-focused sessions don’t appear often in my world, I thought this might be a nice place to start such a conversation.
